Dochase is a marketing technology company leveraging data, insight, channels and tools to enable brands scale.
Over the years, we have grown to be the pioneer of Ad exchange in Africa, leader in advertising tech industry, setting and improving industry standards.

As an Ad exchange, Dochase gives local publishers the opportunity to interface with various advertisers worldwide and vice versa. Many brands trust Dochase for effective advertising campaigns in Africa, America and Europe.
